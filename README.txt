Blue Armadillo Project 

Started in 2015 by a UNF Student Senior Design Project

Members:
	Alejandro Lepervanche
	Elizabeth Voelkel
	And more who helped as much as they could

The Blue Armadillo is a app controlled robot "big cat" toy designed for the Jacksonville Zoo. 

This particular project is the reimplementation of the drive control system in C++ and with a simple PD control loop
